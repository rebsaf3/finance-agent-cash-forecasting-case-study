# Public Redaction Checklist
Use this list before publishing.

1. Remove tenant names and tenant ids
2. Remove internal urls and share links
3. Remove bank names, account numbers, and payment details
4. Remove proprietary financial values, forecasts, and actuals
5. Remove user ids, group ids, and connector ids
6. Confirm examples are sanitized and illustrative only
7. If a detail is not required to explain the design, remove it
